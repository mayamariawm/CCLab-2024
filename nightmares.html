Appearance:

It doesn't have an specific appearance cause it depends on your state of mind, sometimes it can be extremely aggresive or extremely passive.

Key Characteristics:

  • It grows as you touch it and times passes
  • Feeds from your touch
  • It doesn't have form
  • Needs a human to survive

What we know so far about NIGHTMARE? In a world where thoughts take shape, there exists a creature known as Nightmares. It lurks in the shadows of our minds, feeding on our deepest fears and insecurities. The more we try to avoid our fears, the stronger Nightmares becomes. You must be careful not to let Nightmares touch you, for each contact causes it to grow even more rapidly, your fears multiplying and intensifying. The creature becomes a swirling mass of your worst thoughts, threatening to consume your entire being. The path is not easy. Sometimes, Nightmares will catch you off guard, growing suddenly when you least expect it. But you persist, knowing that each setback is temporary, each confrontation making you more resilient. In the end, you stand tall, surrounded by the fading whispers of Nightmares, ready to face whatever new challenges your dreams may bring.


Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Please stay 10 meters away form these creature, it's extremely dangerous to human interaction. If for some reason there is contact, please contact 911 inmediately, as you may present various serious sintoms regarding your health.
